Why fly Budapest?

Wide variety of flights

Pick your preferred flight from tons of options. From city shuttles, to some of our longest commercial and cargo flights to the Far East, and everything in between, there is sure to be a flight for you in Budapest.

A few metres away from the real guys

Throughout the event, our ATC stations will be staffed from the building of HungaroControl, the Air Navigation Service Provider of Hungary. Get ready to get as real as it gets!

Community

Join fellow IVAO members from all around the world in this exciting occasion that is sure to attract hundreds.

A unique opportunity

Events of this size happen only once a year. Don't miss out on your chance to get involved!

How to participate?

Click on Flight booking in the menu at the top of the page, and choose from our vast selection of flights! Haven't found the ideal flight? No problem, request a private slot!

Remember – this is a Real Flight Event! That means you are expected to fly your exact flight! Make sure to fly the right aicraft, use the correct callsign, depart/arrive at your assigned gate, and depart/arrive as close to on-time as possible!
Simulating real flights is what makes this kind of event so much fun.
