Why to join our event?

Wide variety of flights

By participating on this event you'll have the opportunity to accomplish one (or more) of our many flights.

Realistic ATC service

During the event as much ATC stations will be staffed as possible and required to simulate realistic operations in our professional, high-quality manner.

Different people

Every IVAO member can book his/her flight(s) and participate on this event. It's an opportunity to meet new people from different divisions to pay homage to the same passion.

A unique opportunity

We don't organize many RFE's, so this is your moment to participate on this unique opportunity.

How to participate?

Look at the menu above, and click Flight booking! Haven't found any ideal flights? No problem, request a private slot!

Before going online, don't forget to read the flight briefing, and we strongly recommend to keep it at your fingertips during your flight!
