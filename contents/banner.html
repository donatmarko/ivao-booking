Why to join our event?

Wide variety of flights

By participating in this event, you'll have the opportunity to complete one or more of our many flights.

Top-notch ATC service

Throughout the event, as many ATC stations as possible will be staffed to simulate realistic operations in our professional, high-quality manner.

Different people

Every IVAO member can book their flight(s) and participate in this event. It's an opportunity to meet new people from different divisions who share the same passion.

A unique opportunity

We don't organize many of such events, so this is your chance to participate in this special occasion.

To participate:

Look at the menu above, and click on Flight booking! Haven't found the ideal flight? No problem, request a private slot!

Before going online, don't forget to read the flight briefing. We strongly recommend keeping it at your fingertips during your flight!
